About Us

DU_Black_Noodles is an elite cybersecurity team specializing in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. We focus on ethical hacking, reverse engineering, cryptography, and penetration testing.

Our Mission

  • To push the boundaries of cybersecurity knowledge and ethical hacking.
  • To participate and dominate global cybersecurity competitions.
  • To train and mentor upcoming cybersecurity enthusiasts.
  • To contribute to the open-source and cybersecurity communities.
